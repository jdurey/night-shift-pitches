# Prototype Spec: Vimi

## Visual Constraints
- **Color Palette:** Professional, math-oriented (deep blues, clean whites, subtle purple accents for "AI").
- **Layout:** Two-column split view. Left side: The linear curriculum map. Right side: The hidden "Misconception Substrate" that intercepts students.
- **Disclaimers:** A highly visible badge stating "Simulated Data / Architectural Mockup" at the top of the UI. No Vimi logo; use a generic "Vimi Architecture Demo" header.

## Interactions
- **Clicking a Lesson:** When clicking a linear math standard on the left (e.g., "Fractions: Common Denominators"), the right panel reveals the underlying harvested misconceptions (e.g., "Student adds denominators instead of finding LCM").
- **Intervention Path:** Shows how the AI tutor would dynamically route a student based on a specific misconception trigger, rather than just offering a generic hint.

## Simulated Data Payload
- **Domain:** K-8 Math (Fractions & Decimals).
- **Misconceptions:** Real, evidence-grounded distractors harvested from public released assessments.

## CTA
- "This misconception layer is what turns a generic LLM tutor into a rigorous instructional engine. Let's discuss."
