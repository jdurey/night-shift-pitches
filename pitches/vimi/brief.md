# Pitch Brief: Vimi

## Target Information
- **Company:** Vimi (K-12 AI math tutor; $12M seed)
- **Target Role:** Founder / Head of Curriculum
- **Pain Point:** They claim the AI "adapts to school curriculum," but true adaptivity requires a sequenced knowledge spine and misconception-triggered interventions. Without a rigorous, evidence-grounded misconception substrate, "adaptive" just means randomly jumping around unsequenced math problems.

## Strategy
- **Why Josh:** I have already built an evidence-grounded K-8 misconception bank harvested from released assessment data. I know how to use misconceptions as the foundational substrate of a learning path, rather than just post-hoc hints.
- **Archetype Selection:** Dashboard Prototype (Interactive UI showing a misconception-based adaptive sequence).
- **Proof from Ledger:** K-8 misconception bank (4 domains), harvested from released-assessment data.
- **What not to claim:** Do not claim we have Vimi's actual math data or that we know their codebase. Present this strictly as a conceptual knowledge architecture layer.
